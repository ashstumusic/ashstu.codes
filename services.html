Operate

$250 /mo Maintain clients: no setup fee

For restaurants, cafes, and any business running POS hardware, delivery platforms, and staff scheduling. This package goes beyond the website into the systems that run your operation day to day. You run the business. I run the digital infrastructure behind it.

What's included

  • Everything in Maintain — custom site, hosting, monitoring, directories, brand assets, same-day support
  • POS System Setup — hardware installation, full menu build, modifier groups, and online ordering configured on Toast, Square, or Clover. You purchase hardware directly from the platform; this is labor only.
  • Tip pooling automation — auto-calculated splits by clock-in overlap, with full tax and fee deductions
  • Staff scheduling platform setup and ongoing support — ScheduleFly, 7shifts, or custom-built
  • Hiring & digital onboarding — e-signature forms, compliance docs, reusable onboarding packets
  • Management reporting — automated weekly reports delivered to ownership
  • DoorDash storefront rebuild and optimization
  • Quarterly photography shoot — place, atmosphere, and product shots formatted for all platforms
  • 30-day social content calendar built and scheduled each month
What I do each month
  • All Maintain activities — updates, monitoring, directories, brand
  • POS menu, pricing, and modifier updates within 24 hours
  • Tip pool calculation review — discrepancies flagged and corrected
  • Scheduling platform support for staff additions and changes
  • Weekly management reports delivered to ownership
  • Build and schedule next 30 days of social content
  • Optimize DoorDash listing based on order data
  • Quarterly: full photography shoot, new content assets produced

Platform note: POS hardware, software subscriptions, and transaction fees are paid directly by you to the platform (Toast, Square, or Clover). Scheduling platform subscriptions (ScheduleFly, 7shifts) are also paid directly to those providers. My fee covers setup labor and ongoing management — not third-party costs.

Your role Run your business. Call me when something needs to change.
